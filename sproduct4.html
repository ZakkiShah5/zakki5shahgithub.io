Product Details

Biscotti are one of my favorite sweet indulgences. What's not to like? They're easy to make, very satisfying and relatively light and healthy since they're lower in fat and sugar than most cookies. They're also usually packed with heart healthy nuts.
Biscotti are traditional Italian pastries. Classic biscotti cookies are dry and crisp, so they are ideal for dipping in hot coffee or tea. Sometimes biscotti are dipped in melted chocolate. Biscotti are baked twice, which gives them their dry, crunchy texture.
